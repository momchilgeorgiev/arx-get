# Privacy Policy for arx-get

**Last Updated: December 31, 2024**

## Overview

arx-get is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data the extension collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data.

## Data Collection

arx-get collects minimal data necessary for its functionality:

### What We Collect
- **Whitespace Preference**: Your choice of whitespace replacement (underscore, hyphen, or spaces) for PDF filenames

### What We DO NOT Collect
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Downloaded paper titles or content
- arXiv papers you view or download
- IP addresses
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Any data that could identify you

## Data Storage

- Your whitespace preference is stored **locally** in your browser using Chrome's `chrome.storage.sync` API
- This data never leaves your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties

## Data Usage

The single piece of data we store (your whitespace preference) is used solely to:
- Remember your preferred filename format
- Apply it automatically to future downloads

## Permissions

arx-get requires the following permissions:

- **activeTab**: To read the current arXiv page URL and identify which paper you're viewing
- **tabs**: To list open arXiv tabs for bulk downloads
- **downloads**: To download PDF files with custom filenames
- **storage**: To save your whitespace preference locally
- **host permission (arxiv.org)**: To extract paper titles from arXiv pages
- **content access (scholar.google.com)**: To read Google Scholar result pages and find arXiv links

These permissions are used **only** for the extension's core functionality and nothing else.

## Third-Party Access

- arx-get does **not** share data with third parties
- arx-get does **not** use analytics services
- arx-get does **not** contain ads or trackers
- arx-get does **not** use remote code or scripts

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Clear your stored preference at any time by uninstalling the extension
- Use the extension without creating an account
- Access the source code (available on request)

## Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by arx-get:
1. Right-click the extension icon
2. Select "Remove from Chrome" or "Manage extension"
3. Click "Remove"

This will permanently delete your stored whitespace preference.
