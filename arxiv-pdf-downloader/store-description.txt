arx-get - Download arXiv PDFs with meaningful filenames

Stop downloading papers with cryptic names like "2401.02524v2.pdf"

arx-get automatically renames arXiv PDFs to their actual paper titles, making your research library instantly searchable and organized.

FEATURES:
• Works on both abstract and PDF pages
• Bulk download from open arXiv tabs
• Pulls arXiv links from Google Scholar results
• Extracts actual paper titles from arXiv metadata
• Customizable filename format (underscores, hyphens, or spaces)
• Remembers your preferences
• Fast and lightweight
• No tracking or data collection

EXAMPLE:
Without arx-get: 2401.02524v2.pdf
With arx-get: Attention_Is_All_You_Need.pdf

HOW TO USE:
1. Navigate to any arXiv paper
2. Click the arx-get icon
3. Choose your preferred whitespace format
4. Click "Download current tab"

BULK DOWNLOAD:
1. Open multiple arXiv tabs or a Google Scholar results page
2. Click the arx-get icon
3. Select papers and click "Download selected"

That's it! Your paper downloads with a meaningful filename.

Perfect for researchers, students, and anyone who reads academic papers. Keep your downloads folder organized without manual renaming.

PRIVACY:
arx-get only reads arxiv.org and scholar.google.com pages to find papers and stores your whitespace preference locally. No data is collected or transmitted.

Open source and free forever.
